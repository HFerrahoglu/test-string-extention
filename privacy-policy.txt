# Privacy Policy for Test String Generator

## Data Collection
This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## What We Don't Do
- We don't collect any personal information
- We don't track your browsing history
- We don't store any data on our servers
- We don't use cookies or tracking technologies
- We don't share any information with third parties

## How It Works
- All data generation happens locally in your browser
- No internet connection is required
- All data is stored locally in the extension
- No external API calls are made

## Permissions
The extension requests minimal permissions:
- activeTab: To interact with the current tab
- clipboardRead/clipboardWrite: To copy generated text
